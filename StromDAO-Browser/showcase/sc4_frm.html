Showcase: Accounting using „Stromkonto"

Accounting principals within STROMDAO Energy Blockchain is mainly handled by a concept called Stromkonto (Power Account Statement). Within this experience accounting rules of a smart contract at 0x19BF166624F485f191d82900a5B7bc22Be569895 will be used.
